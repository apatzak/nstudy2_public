To view a Folder in nStudy:

  1. Click nStudy icon then choose" Open Library"


  2. The library view will open. Click the arrow next to "My Library"


  3. A list of all items you created will appear. The most recent created folder will appear at the bottom of the list


  4. A text box will appear. Type in the Folder's title, then click save


  5. Once you click save, "My Library" page will appear




  6. Mod-click anywhere on your Map
  7. Choose "Group" from the context menu



  8. Give your Group a title by typing over "untitled group..."
