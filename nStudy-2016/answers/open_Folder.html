To open a Folder in nStudy:

  1. Click nStudy icon then choose" Open Library"



  2. The library view will open. Click the arrow next to "My Library"


  3. A list of all items you created will appear. The most recent created Folder will appear at the bottom of the list


  4. Click the arrow next to the Folder you want to open


  5. A list of items in the Folder will appear
