To search in the Library:




1. Click on the nStudy icon in your window to open the menu. Choose "Open Library."



2. In the library, you have several search options. You can choose to search just within quotes, just within notes, or across both.
Leaving both "quotes" and "notes" unselected searches across both types. Alternatively, you could select both. To choose both Quotes and Notes: Windows users: press down the "Ctrl" button while clicking on both Quotes and Notes. Mac users: press down the "Command" button while clicking on both Quotes and Notes 3. Type in text you are searching for.

4. You can limit your search with several options. If you are searching for an item that was created within a certain period of time, you can set the beginning and end dates for that period of time.
