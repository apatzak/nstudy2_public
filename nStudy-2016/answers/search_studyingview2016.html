In the studying view, you can search using the side bar.

1. To access the Side bar, click on the double arrows on the left side of your webpage.



2. Type in the search term(s) you are looking for. Numbers to the right of the artifact name (i.e., Quotes, Tags) will indicate how many of those items in each category contain the search term(s).




3. You can click on the triangles next to item categories to open up search results within specific types. (I.e., Tags, Quotes, Notes, Terms)


4. Clicking on a search result opens it up in your studying view.
