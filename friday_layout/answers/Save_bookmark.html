In nStudy, a bookmark refers to a web page you have visited on the Internet and saved.
  1. Mod-click anywhere in the webpage to open the context menu

  2. Choose “Bookmark Page in nStudy” from the context menu
    3. When you quote a text on a bookmark, the webpage is automatically saved under Recent Bookmarks


  3. The bookmark will appear under Recent Bookmarks on nStudy panel
    4. Bookmarks are listed by time, most recent on top


  4. You can rename the webpage title by typing over it
