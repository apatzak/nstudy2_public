If you highlight text in the browser, you can quote that text in the nStudy panel. To add a quote :
  1. Go to any webpage
  2. Click at the beginning of text you want to quote then drag through it to select it
  3. Mod-click anywhere in the text you selected



  4. In the context menu, choose Quote
  5. The quote will appear in the nStudy panel under the bookmark it was quoted from
