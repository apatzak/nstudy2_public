You can create a term by following these steps:
  1. Click on the arrows next to the message entry area


  2. Click the type of prompt you would like to use from the context menu


  3. A box opens with suggested prompts to choose from


  4. Click on the one you want
  5. The prompt will appear in the message entry area


  6. Press eneter, the prompt will appear in the chat panel


    7. OR you can:
    1. Go to any webpage
    2. Select your term
    3. Mod-click anywhere in the text you selected



    4. In the context menu, choose Create Term
    5. The term will open in a popup window
    6. Type in the definition


    7. The term will appear in the nStudy panel under the bookmark it was created from

      8. Tip: If you needed more space, you can double click your “Term” to open it in the main browser.
