1. To copy a Note, mod-click it, then choose "Copy Note" from the popup menu.
    2. A mod-click is a right-click for Windows users and a control-click for Mac users


  2. To paste a Note, mod-click the collection you want to paste to, then choose "Paste Note Here" from the popup menu
    You can only paste obejcts in collections. nStudy Collections are: "My Folder", "Folder", "See also", and "Notes" in "Bookmarks" and "Quotes"
