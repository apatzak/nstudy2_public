You can create a term by following these steps:
  1. Mod-click “My Folders” or any nStudy item
    2. nStudy items are: “Folders”, “Terms”,“Notes”, “Bookmarks”, and "Quotes"

  2. Choose “Term” from the context menu


  3. Type over “untitled term”to write your term


  4. Then click on the triangle pointing to the right
  5. The Term will unfold
  6. Type in the definition of your term over “Enter text here. . . ”


    7. Tip: If you needed more space, you can double click your “Term” to open it in the main browser.


OR you can:
  1. Go to any webpage
  2. Select your term
  3. Mod-click anywhere in the text you selected



  4. In the context menu, choose Create Term
  5. The term will appear in the nStudy panel under the bookmark it was created from
