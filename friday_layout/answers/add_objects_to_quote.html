1. Go to any webpage
  2. Click at the beginning of text you want to quote then drag through it to select it
  3. Mod-click anywhere in the text you selected
  4. Choose “Annotate” instead of “Quote” from the context menu


  5. The quote will open in a popup window
  6. Mod-click anywhere in the quote
  7. Choose the item you want to add from the context menu

    1. Mod-click your "quote" and choose an item from the context menu
