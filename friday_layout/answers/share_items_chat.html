You can share items you saved/created on nStudy by following these steps:
  1. Mod-click the nStudy item you want to share
    2. nStudy items are: “Folders”, “Terms”,“Notes”, “Bookmarks”, and "Quotes"

  2. Choose “Copy” from the context menu



  3. Click “Paste item” on the chat panel


  4. The item will appear right on top of the message entry area


  5. Type in a message, then press enter, the item is shared
