1. Mod-click a collection then choose "Document"
    nStudy Collections are: "My Folders", "Folder", and "Notes" in "bookmark" and "Quotes"

  2. Type over "untitled document" to give your essay a title

  3. Mod-click the essay's title and choose "Open in a New Window", then a popup window will appear

  4. Write your essay in the space provided

Tip: The writing document has features similar to MS Word Editor. Some of these features are:
  1. Insert a table
  2. Insert an Image
