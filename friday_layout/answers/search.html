nStudy allows you to search for items you created. To search for an item:
  1. Click “ List/ Search All Items ”at the top of the nStudy panel



  2. Type in the key word you are looking for in the search box


  3. All items with the key word will appear under the search area.



    4. Tip: You can check the items you want to search in. If you uncheck an item, it won't be included in your search .
