1. Mod-click a collection, then choose "Document"
    A mod-click is a right-click for Windows users and a control-click for Mac users
    nStudy Collections are:" My Folder", "Folder", "See also", and "Notes" in "Bookmarks" and "Quotes"

  2. Type over "untitled document" to give your essay a title

  3. Mod-click the essay's title and choose "Open in a New Window", then a popup window will appear.

  4. Write your essay in the space provided

    5. Tip: The writing document has features similar to MS Word Editor. Some of these features:
    1. Insert a table
    2. Insert an Image
