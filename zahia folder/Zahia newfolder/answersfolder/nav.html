A term has a default template that includes:
  1. Three fields: Term, Definition and Terms Used. The Terms Used field has a special feature. If the description of a term includes any other terms, nStudy automatically lists that other term under the Terms Used header
  2. A collection “See Also” is where you can mod-click to add other items to your term
    3. A mod-click is a right-click for Windows users and a control-click for Mac users How do I create and fill in a term? How do I create objects connected to my term? How do I label my term?
