If you highlight text in the browser, this text is saved as a quote. To add a quote :
  1. Go to any webpage
  2. Click at the beginning of text you want to quote then drag through it to select it
  3. Once you select it, choose “ Highlight ”


  4. A nub for this quote will appear in the gutter on the right end of your webpage


  5. The quote will also appear in “Maps ”inside the bookmark saved



    6. Tip: When you hover over a nub in the gutter, a banner unfolds showing the first few words of the quote you created .


  6. To open your quote in “Maps ”, drag and drop your quote in the map, then click it, the quote will unfold
