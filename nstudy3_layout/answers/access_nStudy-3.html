You can access nStudy by following these steps:
  1. Download Chrome web browser
  2. Open the Chrome web browser.
  3. In the address field, enter this URL: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/nstudy/hlkdbomhhpbnfnocledflilgaehkjfje
  4. A message will appear asking you to install the nStudy add-on. Be sure you follow the steps to install.



  5. Click "Create New Account" and enter a username and password.
  6. Log in using your ID and password.

When you log in, the nStudy side panel appears on the right side of your browser window, after the address field. To open nStudy, click the icon.
