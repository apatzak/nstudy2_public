To tag your quote as “ important ” or “to be reviewed ” . . . etc .,
  1. Select text you want to tag and quote


  2. Once you select, a window will open
  3. Move your cursor to the textbox that has “ tag, tag, tag ”


  4. Choose the tag you want to use from the menu box


  5. The tag will show next to the quote in the gutter on the right end of your webpage


  6. You could create your own tag by typing the name in the textbox, the press enter
