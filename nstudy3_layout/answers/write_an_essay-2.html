You can write an essay by following these steps:

  1. Mod-click anywhere on your Map



  2. Choose “Document” from the context menu


  3. Type over "untitled document" to give your essay a title

  4. Then double-click the icon next to the title of your document to open your essay in a new window .


Tip: An nStudy document has common features for editing text, including bolding/italicizing text and inserting lists.
